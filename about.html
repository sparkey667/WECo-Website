About

Wentworth Environment Collaborative, formerly known as the Green Team, is an active student organization that includes students of many majors who wish to improve Wentworth's sustainability and raise awareness about environmental topics. Here at Wentworth Environmental Collaborative we get students involved with planning sustainable events, creating and working on interdisciplinary projects such as the E bar, and discussing about current environmental issues.

Learn

We offer discussions, lectures, and presentations about the environment, as well as ways to conserve the Wentworth and the Boston Community.

Connect

Our club is a very active student organization that consists of multiple majors who all have the same goal of preserving the community.

Conserve

We design events, competitions, and projects on ways to preserve our community by spreading information about sustainability and recycling.

Promote

We like to promote and inform the students at Wentworth about sustainable living, as well as resuable resources.

Inform

Our club is designed to teach the community of Wentworth and Boston about going green and helping out the planet.

Network

Our club gives students the oppurtunity to reach out to local environment companies and learn how they can save the planet.
